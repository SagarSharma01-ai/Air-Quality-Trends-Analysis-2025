## ğŸ’¡ Key Findings

### 1. The "Very Unhealthy" Reality
For the projected year 2025, Delhi recorded a **Max AQI of 397**. The average AQI hovers around **208**, placing the city consistently in the "Unhealthy" category.

### 2. The Primary Culprit: PM2.5
The analysis confirms that **PM2.5** is the most frequent primary pollutant (Frequency: 68). This indicates combustion-based pollution (vehicles/industry) is the main issue, not just road dust.
